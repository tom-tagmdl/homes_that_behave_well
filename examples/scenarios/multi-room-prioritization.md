# Scenario: Multi-Room Prioritization

## Purpose

Manage issues across multiple rooms.

---

## Scenario

Rooms:

- Living Room: AMBER
- Data Closet: RED

---

## System Behavior

System prioritizes highest risk first.

---

## Concierge Output

"The most urgent issue is in the data closet, where temperature is above safe limits."

---

## Key Principles

- prioritize by severity
- provide clear focus
- avoid overwhelming the user

---

## Final Outcome

User addresses the most critical issue first.
