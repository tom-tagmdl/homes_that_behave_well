# Interaction Flow: Capability Discovery

## Purpose

Help users understand what the system can do.

---

## Scenario

User asks:

"What can I do in this room?"

---

## System Behavior

Concierge queries:

- available assets
- available sensors
- available services
- available controls

---

## Response

"In this room, I can monitor environmental conditions, identify risks to assets, suggest improvements, and help you adjust settings such as humidity and lighting if those systems are available."

---

## Key Principles

- responses must reflect real capabilities
- no assumptions or exaggeration
- clarity over completeness

---

## Final Outcome

User gains confidence in system capabilities.
