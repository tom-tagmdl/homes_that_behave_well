# Interaction Flow: Personalization

## Purpose

Adapt responses based on user role.

---

## Scenario

User asks:

"What is this artwork?"

---

## Responses

Guest:

"This painting is part of a private collection and reflects a natural landscape style."

Owner:

"This painting was acquired in 2010 and is known for its use of light and composition."

---

## Key Principles

- role determines depth of response
- content must be deterministic
- no guessing or personalization drift

---

## Final Outcome

Responses match audience appropriately.
