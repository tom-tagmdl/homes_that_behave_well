# Scenario: Gradual Degradation

## Purpose

Handle slow environmental changes without abrupt alerts.

---

## Scenario

Humidity rises gradually from 48% to 55%.

---

## System Behavior

Initial state:

- GREEN → AMBER (early advisory)

No urgent alert triggered.

---

## Advisory

"Humidity is trending upward and approaching the upper recommended limit."

---

## Progression

If trend continues:

- AMBER → RED

---

## Key Principles

- detect trends early
- avoid immediate escalation
- provide calm guidance

---

## Final Outcome

User is informed early without unnecessary alarm.
